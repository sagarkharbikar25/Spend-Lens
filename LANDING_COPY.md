## FAQ

**1. Is this really free?**  
Yes. No login to run an audit. We ask for email only after you see results, if you want a copy sent to you.

**2. How do you calculate savings?**  
We use published list prices and rule-based plan fit (team vs individual, seat count, use case). Every number is explainable—see `PRICING_DATA.md`.

**3. Do you use AI for the recommendations?**  
No. Math and rules are hardcoded. AI only writes a short personalized summary paragraph.

**4. Is my data stored?**  
Audits can be saved to generate a share link. We do not show your email on public share pages. See our stack in `ARCHITECTURE.md`.

**5. What is Credex?**  
Credex offers discounted AI infrastructure credits for teams with meaningful overspend. High-savings audits may receive follow-up—that is optional.
